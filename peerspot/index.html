PeerSpot: The Buying Intelligence Platform
PeerSpot’s Buying Intelligence Platform is where tech pros go to get practical, reliable information on enterprise tech, so they can be sure what they buy is exactly what they need. Powered by the world’s largest community of enterprise tech buyers, PeerSpot provides in-depth reviews, online forums, direct Q&A support and more, giving professionals the confidence to make the right decision and the happiness of reality meeting expectations.
Challenge
Despite employing multiple marketing strategies such as content marketing, ABM, social media, digital marketing, and demand generation, it was still challenging to differentiate and gain prominence in a fiercely competitive market crowded with many players.
The marketing playbook execution does not necessarily guarantee success in standing out and distinguishing oneself.
Approach
We worked with PeerSpot to create a new strategy and brand identity that emphasizes its leadership position in the enterprise tech market and showcase the power of community. The speech bubble and the cursor is at the heart of PeerSpot's brand and embodies its dedication to delivering innovative, yet informed and dependable solutions for its users.
